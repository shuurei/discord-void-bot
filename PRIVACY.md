# Privacy Policy - Erelya Bot

_Last updated: 29 January 2026_

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data Erelya collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

Erelya may collect the following data to operate properly:

- Discord Account ID
- Discord Guild ID
- Messages or commands sent to the bot  
- Logs of command usage and errors  
- Any data you explicitly provide through bot commands

Erelya **does not collect sensitive personal information** such as real names, addresses, or payment information.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

The collected data is used for:
- Functionality of the bot (commands, features, stats)  
- Debugging and fixing issues  
- Improving the user experience  

Erelya **does not sell or share your data** with third parties, except when legally required.

---

## 3. Data Retention

- Data is stored only as long as necessary for the bot to function properly.  
- Logs may be periodically cleared to maintain performance and privacy.

---

## 4. Your Rights

- You can request deletion of your data by contacting the bot owner.  
- You can stop using the bot at any time, which will cease further data collection.

---

## 5. Security

Erelya takes reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. Use the bot at your own risk.

---

## 6. Changes to Privacy Policy

Erelya may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of major changes through announcements. Continued use of the bot constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
